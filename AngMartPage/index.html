Hello & Welcome!

I grew up in a small town called Oxnard, just about 80 miles north of Los Angeles. I have spent a majority of my professional career in the Construction Management & Accounting fields, but I have always had a passion for art and nature.I started Serenity CMD initially as a Construction/Bookkeeping service, and have begun Hemp Jewelery. I plan on incorporating my Chalk Pastel Art, and Holistic Butters & Sprays.

I am NOT licensed nor do I hold any degrees in any areas. However, life has taught me that living experiences trump book knowledge 80% of the time. So all the information, items and anything I do on here has been researched and/or tested by myself. I feel that sharing my knowledge and wisdom with all those I encounter helps them but also helps me in my personal growth and life journey.Thank You For Your Time & I Hope To Work With You Soon!!
